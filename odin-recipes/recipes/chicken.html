Spicy Curry chicken recipes

Ingredients

  • 1 kg (2.2 lbs) chicken pieces, bone-in and skin-on
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 can (400 ml) coconut milk
  • 2 medium tomatoes, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro leaves, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or pot over medium heat. Add the onions and cook until they turn translucent.
  2. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the skillet and cook for another minute until fragrant.
  3. In a small bowl, mix together the curry powder, turmeric powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, chili powder, and paprika to make a spice blend.
  4. Add the spice blend to the skillet and stir well to coat the onions, garlic, and ginger.
  5. Place the chicken pieces into the skillet and brown them on all sides, about 5-7 minutes.
  6. Pour in the coconut milk and stir to combine with the spices. Bring the mixture to a simmer.
  7. Add the diced tomatoes, green bell pepper, and red bell pepper to the skillet. Stir everything together.
  8. Pour in the chicken broth, cover the skillet, and let it simmer for about 30-40 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and tender.
  9. Check the seasoning and add salt according to your taste.
  10. Once the chicken is cooked, remove from heat and garnish with fresh chopped cilantro leaves.
  11. Serve the spicy curry chicken with steamed rice or naan bread.

Enjoy your delicious and spicy curry chicken!
